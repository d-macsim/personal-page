- For each entry in `education` — same dot/entry pattern as roles:
  - Same wrapper, dot, date span, heading structure
  - Detail line: `<p class="text-sm mt-1" style="color: var(--color-text-muted);">{entry.detail}</p>`

- Mobile (<640px): spine hidden (`hidden sm:block`), dots hidden, no left padding (`pl-0 sm:pl-12`), dates visible as inline span above title
- Desktop (sm+): spine visible, dots visible, content indented with `pl-12`
